# Business Requirements

To make an Odoo invoice report visually closer to common Indonesian invoice documents, the following elements are required:

| No | Requirement | Description |
|----|-------------|-------------|
| 1 | Signature Area | Provide space for handwritten signatures at the bottom of the invoice |
| 2 | Stamp Area | Provide empty space for company stamp |
| 3 | Terbilang | Display the total invoice amount in Indonesian words |
| 4 | Familiar Layout | Adjust report structure to resemble conventional Indonesian invoice format |
